# Subscription Manager

> Track all your recurring charges in one place — streaming services, SaaS tools, gym memberships, and more.

## What is the Subscription Manager?

The Subscription Manager is a dedicated tracker for your recurring payments. Unlike transactions, subscriptions are not automatically detected — you add them manually or via the **"Track this"** button on a Fat Finder finding.

![Subscriptions screen with monthly cost and renewals](../img/subscriptions.jpg)

Each subscription stores:
- Name (Netflix, Spotify, gym…)
- Amount and currency
- Billing cycle (monthly, yearly, quarterly, weekly)
- Next renewal date
- Category (optional)
- Notes (optional)

## Adding a Subscription

**Manually:**
1. Go to **Settings → Subscriptions** (or tap the Subscriptions link on the home screen)
2. Tap the **+** button
3. Fill in the name, amount, billing cycle, and next renewal date
4. Tap **Save**

**From Fat Finder:**
If Fat Finder detects a recurring charge, tap **"Track this subscription"** on the finding card. The name and amount are pre-filled — just confirm the renewal date.

## Monthly Cost Banner

The top of the Subscription Manager shows your **total monthly equivalent cost** — all active subscriptions converted to a monthly figure:
- Monthly subscriptions → amount as-is
- Yearly subscriptions → amount ÷ 12
- Quarterly subscriptions → amount ÷ 3
- Weekly subscriptions → amount × (52 ÷ 12)

## Renewal Countdown

Each subscription shows how many days until the next renewal:
- **Red** — overdue or renewing today
- **Yellow** — renewing within 3 days
- **Normal** — more than 3 days away

## Renewal Notifications

You'll receive a push notification **3 days before** each renewal. To manage this:

1. Go to **Settings → Notifications**
2. Toggle **Subscription renewals** on or off

## Editing and Deactivating

Tap any subscription to open the edit screen. You can:
- Update amount, billing cycle, or renewal date
- Add or change the category
- Toggle **Active / Inactive** — inactive subscriptions stay in the list but are excluded from the monthly total
- Delete the subscription entirely

## Viewer Role

If your account role is **Viewer**, you can see the subscription list but cannot add, edit, or delete subscriptions.
