# AI Response Mode

> Choose how the AI talks to you. Simple explanations for beginners, balanced advice for everyday use, or expert-level financial analysis for professionals.

## Overview

The **AI Response Mode** setting lets you control the tone and detail level of all AI-generated content in the app. This affects AI Chat responses, Spending Stories, AI Insights, and Fat Finder reports.

## How to Change

1. Go to **Settings** (gear icon)
2. Scroll to the **App Settings** section
3. Find **AI Response Style**
4. Tap one of the three options

## Available Modes

### Simple

Best for people new to personal finance.

- Uses everyday language, avoids financial jargon
- Uses analogies and comparisons (e.g., "think of a budget like a jar of cookies")
- Short sentences and bullet points
- Rounds amounts to whole numbers
- Encouraging, supportive tone
- Explains financial terms in parentheses when they must be used

### Balanced (Default)

Good for most users who want clear, practical advice.

- Mix of plain language with basic financial terms
- Explains concepts when first mentioned
- Uses specific numbers but rounds where appropriate
- Actionable and practical recommendations

### Expert

Designed for financially literate users and professionals.

- Uses proper financial terminology (burn rate, cash flow, liquidity, allocation)
- Includes ratios and percentages (savings rate, expense-to-income ratio)
- References benchmarks (50/30/20 rule, recommended emergency fund)
- Data-dense responses: tables, precise numbers, trend comparisons
- Suggests advanced strategies (tax optimization, compound interest projections)
- Professional, analytical tone

## What It Affects

The response mode changes the AI's communication style across **all AI features**:

| Feature | Effect |
|---|---|
| **AI Chat** | Response tone, vocabulary, and detail level |
| **Spending Story** | Narrative style and complexity of insights |
| **AI Insights** | How chart descriptions and suggestions are worded |
| **Fat Finder** | Finding descriptions and action suggestions |
| **Savings Goals** | Plan summary and advice language |

## Persistence

- Your choice is saved to your profile and synced across devices
- It applies immediately to all new AI-generated content
- Previously generated content (cached stories, insights) is not re-generated — tap **Regenerate** on those screens to see the new style

## FAQ

- **Q: Does the response mode affect the accuracy of the analysis?**
  **A:** No. The underlying financial analysis is identical regardless of mode. Only the communication style changes.

- **Q: Can I change it back at any time?**
  **A:** Yes. Switch between modes freely in Settings. The change takes effect immediately for new AI responses.

- **Q: Which mode should I choose?**
  **A:** If you're unsure, start with **Balanced**. Switch to **Simple** if responses feel too technical, or to **Expert** if you want more detailed financial analysis.
