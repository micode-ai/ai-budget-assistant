# Savings Goals

> Set a financial goal, and the AI will build a personalized plan — with monthly contributions, category spending limits, and milestone checkpoints to keep you on track.

## Overview

The **Savings Goals** feature lets you define targets like "Save 20,000 PLN by next summer." The AI analyzes your income, expenses, and spending habits over the past 3 months, then generates a step-by-step plan to help you reach your goal.

## How to Access

- Navigate to **Goals** from the home screen or settings
- The Goals list shows all your active, paused, completed, and failed goals

## Creating a Goal

1. Tap the **+** button on the Goals screen
2. Fill in the form:
   - **Goal Name** — e.g., "Vacation fund", "Emergency savings"
   - **Target Amount** — the amount you want to save
   - **Currency** — select from USD, EUR, PLN, GBP, UAH, RUB, BYN
   - **Deadline** — choose a target date, or use quick-select buttons: 3 months, 6 months, 1 year, 2 years
3. Tap **Create Goal**

The AI automatically generates a savings plan after creation. This uses **2 AI requests** from your monthly allowance.

## AI Plan

After creating a goal, the AI generates a detailed plan with:

### Contribution Amounts
- **Monthly contribution** — how much to set aside per month
- **Weekly contribution** — broken down into weekly amounts

### Feasibility Assessment

The AI evaluates how realistic your goal is:

| Rating | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Easy** | Achievable with minimal lifestyle changes |
| **Moderate** | Requires some spending adjustments |
| **Challenging** | Requires significant cuts to current spending |
| **Unrealistic** | Very unlikely without major income/expense changes |

### Category Spending Limits

A table showing for each spending category:
- **Current** — your average monthly spend
- **Suggested** — the AI's recommended limit
- **Savings** — how much you'd save per month

### Checkpoints

A visual timeline with 3–5 milestones:
- Each checkpoint has a **date**, **target amount**, and **label**
- Reached checkpoints show a green checkmark
- Helps you track whether you're on pace

### Summary

A brief text explanation of the plan with actionable advice tailored to your spending patterns.

## Progress Tracking

The goal detail screen shows:

- **Progress bar** — percentage of target amount saved
- **On Track / Behind** indicator
- **Projected completion date** — when you'll reach the goal at your current pace
- **Monthly needed** — how much you still need to save per month
- **Behind by** — how much you're behind schedule (if applicable)

## Contribution History

Every time you tap **Add Funds**, the app records the deposit as a separate entry in your contribution history. The goal detail screen shows a **Contribution History** section below the progress bar with:

- The amount added and the goal's currency
- The date of each deposit
- A note for contributions made via the AI chat ("AI update")

History starts from when you first use "Add Funds" — earlier manual edits to the current amount are not backfilled. Up to 20 most recent contributions are shown.

## Managing Goals

- **Update amount** — adjust your current savings amount as you save
- **Edit goal** — change name, target, or deadline
- **Pause / Resume** — temporarily pause a goal
- **Regenerate Plan** — get a fresh AI plan based on updated spending data (costs 2 AI requests)
- **Delete** — permanently remove a goal

## Goal Statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Active** | Goal is in progress |
| **Paused** | Temporarily paused |
| **Completed** | Target amount reached |
| **Failed** | Deadline passed without reaching the target |

## AI Cost

| Action | AI Requests |
|---|---|
| Create goal (includes plan generation) | 2 |
| Regenerate plan | 2 |
| View goals / progress | Free |

## FAQ

- **Q: Does the AI update the plan automatically?**
  **A:** No. The plan is generated once when you create the goal. Tap **Regenerate Plan** to get an updated plan based on your latest spending data.

- **Q: Can I have multiple goals at the same time?**
  **A:** Yes, you can create as many goals as you like. Each goal is tracked independently.

- **Q: How does the AI know my spending patterns?**
  **A:** It analyzes your expense and income history for the past 3 months in the currently selected account.

- **Q: Is my goal data shared with anyone?**
  **A:** Goals are tied to your account. On shared accounts, other members with access can see the goals.
