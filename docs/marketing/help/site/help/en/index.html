Help & Guides

How to use AI Budget Assistant, feature by feature.

Getting Started

Download AI Budget Assistant and create your account in under a minute. Set your preferred currency and language, and start tracking your finances right away.

Dashboard

Your financial command center. See your budget status, income, expenses, and wallet balances at a glance, with quick actions to add expenses in one tap. You can show or hide individual sections from [Settings](./11-settings.md) → Dashboard Widgets.

Expenses & Income

View, add, and manage all your transactions. Switch between Expenses and Income tabs, with support for multiple currencies, categories, tags, projects, and expense splits.

Voice Input & Receipt Scanning

Let AI do the work. Speak your expense naturally or photograph a receipt — the app extracts amount, description, merchant, and category automatically.

Budgets

Set spending limits and track your progress in real time. Create budgets for specific categories or distribute your budget across multiple categories, with customizable periods and automatic alert thresholds.

Analytics

Visualize your spending patterns with interactive charts. Filter by time period and currency, drill down into details, and get AI-powered insights to understand your finances better.

AI Chat

Ask questions about your finances in natural language. Get spending analysis, budget advice, and personalized saving tips — powered by AI.

Spending Story

Get an AI-generated narrative report of your finances. See achievements, alerts, and insights presented as an engaging visual story.

Accounts

Organize your finances with separate accounts. Use Personal for individual tracking, Shared for family budgets, and Business for company expenses. Invite members with role-based access control.

Wallet & Currency Exchange

Track balances in multiple currencies and exchange between them with live exchange rates. The wallet automatically updates as you add expenses and income.

Settings

Customize your profile, appearance, dashboard widgets, notifications, and data sync. Change your language, currency, theme, and manage your account preferences.

Subscription Plans

Choose the plan that fits your needs. Start free with full expense tracking, upgrade to Pro for advanced AI features, or go Business for unlimited everything.

Achievements & Gamification

Stay motivated with achievements, daily streaks, XP, and levels. Track your financial habits and earn badges as you build consistency.

Investment Portfolio

Track your investment portfolio with real-time market prices. Monitor stocks, ETFs, cryptocurrencies, bonds, and commodities all in one place.

End-to-End Encryption

Protect your financial data with end-to-end encryption (E2EE). All sensitive information is encrypted on your device before being sent to the server — no one except you (and your shared account members) can read it.

Export & Reports

Generate PDF, Excel, and CSV reports of your finances. View monthly spending digests, create encrypted backups, and receive automated email summaries.

Debts & Loans

Track money you lend and borrow. See who owes you, who you owe, record repayments, and monitor due dates — all integrated with your expenses and income.

Savings Goals

Set a financial goal, and the AI will build a personalized plan — with monthly contributions, category spending limits, and milestone checkpoints to keep you on track.

Fat Finder (Expense Audit)

An AI-powered monthly audit that analyzes your spending, identifies waste — subscriptions, recurring splurges, unnecessary services — and suggests concrete cuts with estimated savings.

AI Response Mode

Choose how the AI talks to you. Simple explanations for beginners, balanced advice for everyday use, or expert-level financial analysis for professionals.

Home Screen Widgets

Add Android widgets to your home screen for instant access to your spending data — or to add expenses without even opening the app.

Chat Bots — Telegram, WhatsApp & Slack

Manage your finances directly from Telegram, WhatsApp, or Slack. Chat with AI, add expenses, scan receipts, and send voice messages — without opening the app.

Scenario Simulator

Drag sliders to adjust your spending and income categories — see exactly how much your savings could change over 3, 6, or 12 months.

Invite Friends — Referral Program

Invite friends, earn bonus AI requests, and unlock milestones. Share your unique referral code and get rewarded when friends actively use the app.

Importing transactions from your bank

Import transactions from a CSV or PDF statement of your bank. Supports mBank, PKO BP, Erste Bank, Alior Bank, Revolut, Wise, and any other bank via the universal column mapper.

Reference Data

Categories, merchants, tags, and projects are the building blocks for organizing your expenses. Manage them all from **Settings → Reference Data**.

Subscription Manager

Track all your recurring charges in one place — streaming services, SaaS tools, gym memberships, and more.

Web App

Use AI Budget Assistant right in your browser at https://ai-budget.pl — no install needed. It shares the same account and data as the mobile app.

Alerts: unusual activity

The app watches your spending and notifies you when something looks unusual — no setup needed. Tap the bell icon on the home screen to see all alerts.

Manage your money with AI

Add expenses by voice or a photo of a receipt, track budgets and savings, together with your family. Start free.
