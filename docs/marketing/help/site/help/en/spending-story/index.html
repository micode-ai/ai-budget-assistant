Spending Story

Get an AI-generated narrative report of your finances. See achievements, alerts, and insights presented as an engaging visual story.

Overview

The Spending Story feature creates a personalized, narrative-style report of your financial activity. It highlights key events, achievements, and areas of concern in an easy-to-read card format.

How to Access

  • From the Analytics tab, tap the View Your Spending Story banner
  • The story generates automatically based on your selected period

Period Toggle

At the top of the Spending Story screen:

  • Week — story about the current week’s finances
  • Month — story about the current month’s finances

Tap to switch between views. The story content updates accordingly.

Story Blocks

The AI generates various types of insight blocks:

Overspending Alert (Warning)

  • Yellow card with warning icon
  • Appears when your spending exceeds your income
  • Example: “Your spending exceeded your income by 8,346.74 PLN. It’s a reminder to review and adjust plans for the upcoming months.”

Under Budget Victory (Achievement)

  • Golden card with trophy icon
  • Appears when you stayed under a budget limit
  • Example: “Congratulations! You stayed under your total life budget limit by 1,325.52 PLN. It’s an impressive feat given the unexpected expenses this month.”

Top Expense to Watch

  • Blue card with lightbulb icon
  • Highlights your highest spending category
  • Example: “The ‘Transport’ category topped your expenses at 6,250 PLN. Consider potential savings in this area for next month.”

Additional Story Elements

  • Category breakdowns with percentages
  • Summary narratives describing your overall financial picture
  • Comparison insights vs. previous periods
  • Charts and visualizations embedded in the story

Actions

  • Regenerate — tap to request a freshly generated story with updated data
  • Share Story — share your spending story with others
  • Last Updated timestamp — shows when the story was last generated

Requirements

  • Pro or Business subscription required — Free plan users see a prompt to upgrade
  • Sufficient transaction data — the app needs enough expenses to generate meaningful insights. If you don’t have enough data, you’ll see: “Not enough data to generate a story yet.”
  • Each story generation uses AI requests from your monthly allowance

FAQ

  • Q: How often should I check my Spending Story? A: The story is most useful at the end of each week or month. Tap Regenerate to get the latest analysis.

  • Q: Can I share my Spending Story? A: Yes, tap Share Story to share it via your device’s sharing options.
