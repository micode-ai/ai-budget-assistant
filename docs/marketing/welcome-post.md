## 🇬🇧 English

```
👋 Hey!

Want to share a project we've been working on for a long time — AI Budget Assistant. It's an app that helps you keep your finances under control without spreadsheets, without nagging, and without guilt over every coffee ☕

What it can do:
💬 Understands plain language — just type "spent 250 on groceries" or say it out loud, and the expense lands in the right category automatically
📸 Snap a receipt — the app reads it for you
📊 Shows where your money actually goes — by category, day, project
🎯 Helps set budgets and goals, and gently nudges you when it's time to slow down
🤝 Family accounts — invite your partner, spouse or kids to a shared budget. Everyone adds their own expenses, everyone sees the full picture. Flexible roles: some manage, some just view
🌍 Works in 8 languages and with multiple currencies (USD, EUR, GBP, PLN, UAH and more)
📱 Works offline too — data syncs as soon as you're back online

And one more thing — we have a Telegram bot: log expenses right in chat, by voice or photo of a receipt.

Would love it if you gave it a try and told me what you think 🙌
```
