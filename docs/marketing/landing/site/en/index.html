Frequently asked questions

Is the budget app free?

Yes, the core features are free. Pro raises the AI limits and unlocks advanced analytics.

How do I add expenses?

By voice, a photo of a receipt, or manually. The AI reads the items, total and merchant.

Can I budget with my family?

Yes. A shared account lets everyone see the same expenses and budget in real time.

Can I import bank transactions?

Yes, from Wise and banks via CSV or PDF, with automatic duplicate detection.
