How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your account directly from the app. Follow these steps:

  1. Open AI Budget Assistant on your device
  2. Go to Settings (gear icon in the bottom tab or home screen)
  3. Tap Profile
  4. Scroll down and tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm the deletion when prompted

What Data Is Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your user profile (name, email, preferences)
  • All expense and income records
  • Budgets, categories, tags, and projects
  • Wallet and exchange data
  • AI chat history and usage logs
  • Subscription and payment metadata
  • Push notification tokens
  • All data in shared accounts you own
Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Please make sure to export any data you need before deleting your account.

Data Retention

After account deletion:

  • Your personal data is deleted immediately from our servers
  • Anonymized, aggregated analytics data may be retained for service improvement
  • Stripe payment records are retained by Stripe per their data retention policy
  • Local data on your device (SQLite database) remains until you uninstall the app
